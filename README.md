This project is a fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze BMW car sales across multiple dimensions. It provides clear visual insights on revenue, sales volume, top-selling models, regional performance, and year-over-year growth.

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the revenue trend of BMW car sales from 2010 to 2024?
- Which year recorded the highest revenue, and how does the current selected year compare?
- What is the revenue growth or decline percentage compared to the best sales year?
- Which BMW model has the highest sales volume?
- How do different engine types (Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid) impact sales and revenue?
- Which regions contribute the most to total quantity sold?
- What are the most popular car colors based on sales data?
- What is the average price and revenue performance across different regions?
- How does the quantity sold vary across models, regions, and colors?
- What are the overall sales and revenue patterns for decision-making?
